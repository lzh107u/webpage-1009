Conference Banquet : Shanghai Pavilion

Located on the 38th floor of Shangri-La Hotel, Shanghai Pavilion is the highest restaurant in Tainan. With splendid scenery of Tainan City, Shanghai Pavilion presents authentic Shanghainese and Taiwanese cuisines, and Cantonese dim sum, which makes a fabulous combination of enjoyment.

Chimei Museum

Established by the founder of Chimei Corporation named Hsu Wen-lung, the Chimei Museum is the private museum with richest collection in Taiwan.

Source: Wikipidea

Source: Wikipidea

Open Ceremony: Nezha

As a protection diety in the Chinese folk lore, the official name of Nezha is "Marshal of Central Altar". It's believed that the origin of Nezha was based on Hindu mythology and it was spread into China with Buddhism culture. Combined with traditional folk dance and modern pop music, a new subcultural dance performance called "Techno Prince Nezha" is now popular in Taiwan.

Source: Wikipidea

Chihkan Tower

Built as Fort Provintia by the Dutch in 1652, it was originally the location of the administrative center of the United East India Company(VOC). Most of its structure that we can see nowadays was added or modified by the Chinese in the past 300 years.

Anping Old Fort

The Anping Fort, originally named "Fort Zeelandia", was built in 1624 by a combined military and commercial force of the United East India Company(VOC) as a defensive stronghold.

Source: Wikipidea

Source: Wikipidea

Confucian Temple

Named after one of the greatest educator and philosopher in Chinese history called Confucius, the Confucian Temple was built in 1666 during the Koxinya Dynasty as a religious and educational site. Nowadays, students around Taiwan still come to Confucian Temple to pray for good luck on their tests.

National Museum of Taiwan Literature

Opened in 2003, the NMTL is a significant cultural center, which is dedicated to collecting, researching and promoting Taiwanese literature. The building was built in 1916 during the Japanese rule of Taiwan, served as the administration center in Tainan.

Source: Wikipidea

Nabeyaki Egg Noodle

The dish gets its name with the specific cooking style in which the ingredients are cooked and served in small clay pots. It's called "Kuo Shao Yi Mian" in Taiwanese.

Khong bah png (braised pork rice)

They are typically made of pork belly and served on top of rice. It was originally a Fujianese cuisine from Quanzhou, China and was brought to Taiwan by immigrants.

Apricot Kernel Tofu

Also called Annin Tofu or Xingren Tofu, it's a soft, jellied traditional Beijing summer dessert made of apricot kernel milk, agar and sugar.

Source: Tainan City Gov

Shaved Ice

It's a ice-based desserts made of fine shavings of ice or finely crushed ice and sweet condiments or syrups. In Tainan, fruit, condensed milk and tapioca are often added into shaved ice to have a better flavor.
